# Steve 4. Tu
Steve 4. Tu (he/him/ä»–) is a SSHRC CGS-D and Digital Research Alliance of Canada-funded PhD student at the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education at the University of Toronto. His research interests include philosophy and theory of higher education, multispecies ethnography, other-than-human stories, environmental & posthumanities, "artificial" intelligence, student mental health, post-qualitative inquiry, research-creation, terror management theory, possibility studies, and anti-Asian racism.
